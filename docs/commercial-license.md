# SolGuard Commercial Licensing

> This page explains how commercial use works. It is not legal advice. Final terms belong in a signed agreement.

## English

SolGuard is published under Business Source License 1.1 (BUSL-1.1). The public license covers evaluation, internal testing, and other non-production uses allowed by the license text. If you want to run SolGuard in production or use it in a paid product or service, you need a commercial license.

If that is your use case, email [contact@altlayer.io](mailto:contact@altlayer.io) and we will scope the license with you. Please include your company or protocol name, intended use, whether you need redistribution rights, expected users or deployments, and the support level you want.

### Commercial terms

A commercial license can include:
- production deployment rights
- internal team use
- redistribution rights for approved environments
- priority support
- integration help

### Common use cases

Teams usually ask for a commercial license when they want to:
- run SolGuard inside a SaaS product
- ship it as part of a paid security platform
- use it across an internal security team
- redistribute it to affiliates, contractors, or managed service customers

### Why BUSL-1.1

BUSL-1.1 keeps the repository public and puts commercial use behind a paid license. That keeps evaluation simple and makes the production boundary explicit.

### How to buy

Email [contact@altlayer.io](mailto:contact@altlayer.io) with:
- your company or protocol name
- how you want to use SolGuard
- whether you need redistribution rights
- the number of users or deployments
- the support level you want

We will reply with a scope and a draft order form.

### If you only want to evaluate

You can still review the code, test the workflow, and validate fit before buying a license. The public license already covers that.

## 中文

SolGuard 使用 Business Source License 1.1（BUSL-1.1）发布。公共许可证覆盖评估、内部测试，以及许可证文本允许的非生产用途。如果你要把 SolGuard 用在生产环境，或者放进收费产品和服务里，就需要商业授权。

如果是这种用途，直接发邮件到 [contact@altlayer.io](mailto:contact@altlayer.io)，我们会一起确认授权范围。

### 商业条款

商业授权通常包括：
- 生产环境部署权
- 团队内部使用权
- 在约定环境内的再分发权
- 优先支持
- 集成协助

### 常见购买场景

通常会来问商业授权的团队有几类：
- 把 SolGuard 放进 SaaS 产品
- 作为收费安全平台的一部分对外提供
- 给内部安全团队统一使用
- 分发给关联方、外包方或托管客户

### 为什么用 BUSL-1.1

BUSL-1.1 保留了公共仓库的可见性，同时把商用权留给付费使用者。评估和生产部署分开，边界更清楚。

### 如何购买

发邮件到 [contact@altlayer.io](mailto:contact@altlayer.io)，写清楚：
- 公司或协议名称
- 你打算怎么使用 SolGuard
- 是否需要再分发权
- 预期用户数或部署数
- 需要什么级别的支持

我们会根据这些信息发你授权范围和订单草案。

### 如果你只是想先评估

你可以先看代码、试流程、确认是否适合。公共许可证已经覆盖这部分用途。
